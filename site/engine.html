How to read a prediction. The bar shows each outcome's probability — win, draw, or win. The "most likely score" is the single most probable exact scoreline. These can disagree: a model can say South Korea 45% to win yet call 1–1 the likeliest score, because 1–1 is the biggest single result while South Korea's many winning scores (1–0, 2–1, 2–0…) add up to more. The bar answers "who wins?"; the score answers "what's the single most likely final score?". The 🌡 💤 ✈️ badges mark heat, rest and travel adjustments.
